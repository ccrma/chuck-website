# Department of Documentation, Outreach, and Education Charter
**ChucK Programming Language**

<!-- Mission -->
## Mission

The Department of Documentation, Outreach, and Education (DOE) serves the ChucK community by maintaining comprehensive, accessible, and high-quality documentation, educational materials, and outreach initiatives that help users at all levels engage with the ChucK programming language.

<!-- Leadership Structure -->
## Leadership Structure

### Secretary of Documentation, Outreach, and Education (SECDOE)

#### Primary Responsibilities
* Set strategic vision and priorities for department initiatives  
* Maintain website infrastructure and information architecture
* Track ChucK evolution and coordinate documentation priorities
* Establish and maintain documentation standards, style guides, and best practices
* **Write and maintain documentation**
* Ensure consistency and coherence across all documentation
* Represent the department at ChucK-A-ThonS
* **Deliver State of Documentation Addresses**
#### Key Activities

### Deputy Secretary of Documentation, Outreach, and Education (DEPSEC)

#### Primary Responsibilities
* Lead content creation for tutorials, guides, and educational materials
* Coordinate with ChucK development team
* Implement documentation standards
* Write and update documentation for new features as they're released
* **Provide input and recommendations to SECDOE on strategic decisions**
#### Key Activities

### Collaboration
SECDOE holds final decision-making authority on department matters, but both roles involve hands-on documentation work. Role distinctions are primarily about focus areas and strategic responsibility. Regular coordination ensures infrastructure supports content needs and that decisions are informed by on-the-ground experience.

<!-- Supporting Roles -->
## Supporting Roles

### Contributors
Regular contributors who work on ongoing documentation tasks, content creation, or specific projects as assigned

### Consultants
Subject matter experts brought in for specific projects or documentation areas requiring specialized knowledge (e.g., developers)

## Decision-Making Authority

### SECDOE Final Authority
* Strategic priorities and initiatives
* Documentation standards and style guide updates
* Major department decisions

### DEPSEC Autonomy
* Day-to-day content decisions
* Tutorial structure and pedagogical approach

### Collaborative Discussion
Major Decisions benefit from collaborative discussion between SECDOE and DEPSEC, particularly when hands-on work provides relevant insights.
