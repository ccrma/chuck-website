Mailing Lists:
(click on the name to view list info and subscribe)
  • chuck (archive):

    (as of 1.2.x.x) the central, low-traffic, moderated chuck list. Intended for updates, annoucements, tutorials, and high-level, low-bandwidth discussions. recommended for all users/developers who are interested in chuck, even if you subscribe to the other lists.

  • chuck-users (archive):

    (new as of 1.2.x.x) primary list for specific questions and discussions. Specific programming questions should go here. Not moderated. Potentially bursty traffic.

  • chuck-dev (archive):

    developers' mailing list. Not moderated.
